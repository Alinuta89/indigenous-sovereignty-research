Treaty Acknowledgment

This research concerns Treaties 6, 7, and 8 — agreements between the Crown and Blackfoot Confederacy (Siksika, Piikani, Kainai), Tsuut'ina Nation, Stoney Nakoda (Bearspaw, Chiniki, Goodstoney), Cree Nations (including Ermineskin, Samson, Louis Bull, Montana), Dene, and Chipewyan Nations. Treaty Nations are prior-authority parties with sovereign claims. The fiscal flows analyzed here move through treaty territory under authority that derives from these treaty relationships.

This analysis uses publicly available data and published sources. OCAP (Ownership, Control, Access, Possession) principles are observed throughout. No Nation-level data is used without verifying public availability. Indigenous positions are cited from Indigenous sources.
