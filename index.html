Indigenous Sovereignty & Funding Research

Evidence base for Treaty Nations, fiscal architecture, and service capacity across Alberta
This research concerns Treaties 6 (1876), 7 (1877), and 8 (1899). Treaty Nations are prior-authority parties with sovereign claims that predate Alberta (1905). All analysis, intervention designs, and policy proposals are produced for Nation consideration, not as prescriptions.
