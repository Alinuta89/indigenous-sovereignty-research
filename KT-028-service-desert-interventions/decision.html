Binding constraint: The workforce does not exist. Best case: 7–10 years to capacity. Likely: 10–15 years.

Funding principle: No single source above 40%. The 84% single-funder dependency must not be recreated.

Community authority: No intervention is valid without Nation-level engagement. This is analysis, not a plan.

Stress Test

CONDITIONAL PASS — 2 PASS, 7 CONDITIONAL, 0 FAIL across 9 scenarios. The analysis survives all challenges with caveats on community engagement, cost precision, workforce timeline, and administrative burden of blended funding.

What This Is Not

This is analysis, not a plan. The absence of formal services does not mean the absence of community care — kinship networks, ceremony-based healing, mutual aid, and land-based wellness sustain communities outside the 922-organization map. These proposals address the formal institutional gap. They must support, not replace, community-level care systems.
