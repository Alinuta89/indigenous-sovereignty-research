Research — KT-030

This research concerns Treaties 6 (1876), 7 (1877), and 8 (1899) — agreements between the Crown and sovereign First Nations that predate the Province of Alberta (1905). The funding networks analyzed flow through treaty territory. Treaty Nations are prior-authority parties with sovereign claims, not stakeholders in a funding ecosystem.
